Tema Municipality Chamber

Join the Chamber

Tema Municipality Chamber of Commerce gives you the opportuniity to register your business and enjoy the benefits of quality advertisements, promotions and belonging. Businesses in our chamber feel a sense of belonging to the municipality. Click the button below to be part of this great community.

Purpose

The sole purpose of this chamber is to promote the interests of the members of the chamber oranization as well as promote the municipality, attracting members of the communities.

Goals

Our goal is to see all local businesses in the municipality achieve their advertisement goals through our platform and give them the opportunity to fulfill some of their civic responsibilites in the society. The chamber of commerce wishes to be a unique place where the attractions of the municipality are promoted to create awareness of the beauty of Tema.

A little Tour in the Municipality

Tema is a wonderful city located on the atlantic coast of Ghana. It is referred to as the "harbour city" because of the busiest harbour and seaport we find there. The city can boast of many social amenities. There are twenty five(5) communities and many townships in Tema. All the townships and communities in the city have basic access to social amenities. This makes your stay in Tema a lovely one. Check out some pictures of a few loving locations in the municipality.
