A little about Fish Haven

by Logan Howlett 1 day ago

Founders Day Fresco is an event that is held at Foundation park every year. I have been taking the festival for granted and have not been properly engaging in perhaps one of the most popular community events of the year. In response, I contacted Mr. Kentucky, a representative via email and expressed my desire to serve on the event committee. Well, you guessed it, the phone rings almost immediately and she promptly asks me to be the event committee chair. Literally, good grief! My appreciation for the years of dedicated, generous service of volunteers and sponsors has escalated beyond what I thought I could feel for such a thing and the effort I can put in to render a willing to the committee and people. The people in the community deserve to be given the best of everything if not every where, their own community should stand up for what they are supposed to get. It is an an opportunity for me to help the committee and the the community to serve the people. It is amazing working in the committee, especially meeting new people of great worth, investing all kinds of into the happiness of the community. I love doing what I am doing now and I can boldly say that the Founder's Day Fresco event held at the founder's park is one of the best, if not the best in Idah.
