Fitt's Law

Family Search

Familysearch.org

The design principle on this page is quite clear to me. In my opinion, the size of the sign up button brings it close so it can be seen and used easily by those who wish to register for an account. Althoh there is additionally a clear text hierarchy of which the the button is part but I see that the Fitt's law principle stands out.

PARC: Alignment

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day saints

ChurchofJesusChrist.org

Items on this page are aligned with one another in a way that the organization of items improves navigating experience. The elements on the homepage are vertically and horizontally aligned in a way that the whitespaces between them are moderately kept.

PARC: Contrast

WordPress

Wordpress.com

In my opinion, the homepage has been designed to show a distinct difference between the color of the text and the background. This effectively improves readerbility. Also the contrast principle exemplified on this page deals with visual hierarchy of font-sizes.
