Target Audience Statement

Description

The website is basically targetted at parents and students in the community. Parents in the community will want to know the weather condition and plan how their families can cope. Students in the community are inquisitive to know the weather condition so they are prepared for any unwanted weather condition.

Personas

Target Age Group Education
Parents 27- 68 High School/College Graduates
Students 16- 25 High School/College

Scenarios

The main purpose of our audience visiting the site is to be provided with weather information. Our audience would like to use the site to stay updated about weather conditions. Their questions would be mainly about the weather condition in their areas at a specific time. Below are some scenario examples:

  • What's the weather going to be like this weekend at my area?
  • Is the forecast going to be the same as I predicted for my on Friday?
  • What is the weather going to be like in some weeks?
  • What are the implications of this weather forecasted for Monday?
  • How long is this weather condition going to last?
